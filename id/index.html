Indonesia is the 2nd rank produces more than 75% of global tropic seaweed (Food and Agriculture Organization, 2022) and. Seaweed is an alternative seafood that can be processed. The most important products processed from these seaweeds are the hydrocolloids Agar and Carrageenan. In Indonesia, the use of seaweed started from the agar industry (Gelidium / Gelidiella and Gracilaria). Carrageenan produced from the raw material (Eucheuma).

How to Order?

Minim order 15 MT

The minimum quantity for placing an order is 15 metric tons (MT). Orders below this quantity cannot be processed, so please ensure your order meets this requirement.

Quotation Method: CNF / TT

We provide quotations based on CNF (Cost and Freight) or TT (Telegraphic Transfer). CNF covers the cost of goods and freight charges to the destination port, while TT is the payment method used for the transaction.
